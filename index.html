I’m a Scientific Researcher at the fortiss research institute, and a PhD candidate at the Technical University of Munich, Chair of Software and Systems Engineering.

My research lies at the intersection of assurance, knowledge representation & reasoning on one side, and the software, artificial intelligence & hybrid systems on the other. I'm also interested in other properties ( alignment, regulatory compliance, trustworthiness, adversarial robustness ) related to novel architectures ( foundation models, compound AI, neurosymbolic AI ).

This website is a clearinghouse for my work and ideas. I try to make all my publications available on arXiv and ResearchGate. Any comments, reviews, critiques, or objections are invited and should be sent to me by e-mail.
